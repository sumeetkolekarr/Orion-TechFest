ABOUT US

ORION, a student-driven festival is the jewel and pride of our university. Ranked among Odisha's Top 5 College Fests, ORION is a student-driven fest working for the past few years. Today, ORION is one of Odisha's biggest college festivals. It brings together a vibrant mix of culture, creativity, and competition, offering an electrifying experience that thrills and pushes for high-energy performance. Orion features everything from street plays and dance-offs to runway shows, making it an unforgettable celebration of talent and passion!
